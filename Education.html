    OVERVIEW

    It’s time to reconsider education. Today, 32 million Indian children of age up to 13 years have never attended any school, the majority of them belonging to the socially disadvantaged class. So how we can provide education to maximum children living in rural / urban areas in less amount and by proving facilities similar to that of private school?

    • We can’t control the increasing population. Fortunately, we do not have to—we have to open school in each and every rural/urban area which will not only provide quality education but also employment to many educated unemployed people around the country.
    • In the near-term, it is seen that many schools have been opened in different parts of country for all, but it doesn’t reach everyone who needs it. Today, India has almost 3-4 times the number of schools than China despite a similar population. Nearly 4 lakh schools have less than 50 students and a maximum of two teachers each. Around 1.5 crore Indian students’ study in such unprivileged school.
    • Ek Pehel Anant Tak Foundation works not only on encouraging the students to learn from the experiences of others and increase their communication skills, but also provides a way of capturing the way of life that may disappear from our world as megacities start to take over.

    WHY IT MATTERS?

    Education plays a critical role in development, as it is a key factor in developing the lives of people living in the community. With education, there is always a bright future in store for communities. As a student and educator, We believe that the past shows us that education is an important factor in bringing about rural development in any country.

    WHAT ARE WE DOING?

    Ideated by four passionate friends from different parts of country, this youth-driven nonprofit organization started in 2021. Currently, the organization works with youth in 3 cities with the help of an impressive 100 plus strong volunteer base.It strives to ensure that even the most vulnerable children in shelters are able to realize their dreams and break the vicious circle of poverty, through its interventions which Empower Children, Improve the Ecosystem, and Enable the Sector.

    • Volunteers provide educational and mentoring support, and help build the children’s emotional health. You can make a difference here. Anant Mittal, the founder of Ek Pehel Anant Tak - Foundation, believes that only a people’s movement can ensure children’s quality education .
    • He enable the holistic development of students, and also make an impact on the school and community through his initiatives. Our next project has grown in scope and reach with a focus on high quality, low cost, and replicable interventions which addresses the lapses in the education system.
    • With a mission of ‘Every child in school and learning well’ the organization has addressed several issues such as learning levels, dropout rates, child rights, and teacher training. Our programs focus on education, vocational training, technology, vulnerable children, and research and advocacy.
